About Royal Sumatra


Royal Sumatra is the largest residence in the city of Medan. With a total area of ​​300 hectares and 92 hectares of Green area,
Royal Sumatra is committed to maintaining sustainability and greening where you can enjoy comfortable, beautiful, healthy living and away from pollution and city fatigue.
Royal sumatra is the right choice, for those who are looking for a quiet environment and luxurious living Enjoy life in harmony, together with Royal Sumatra.

The Royal Botanica Park area is equipped with various Community Hub facilities in the form of a lake with facilities such as:
-Multi-Purpose Pavilion, Swimming Pool with lake view, Children Playground, Yoga & Morning Deck, Promenade and Jogging Track.

In addition, Royal Sumatra also has supporting facilities such as:
- Commercial center 750m long
- Restaurants and clubhouses
- 18-hole international Golf Course
- SIS International School
- Akasha Park (Open space gathering hub)


With access:
5 minutes to Carrefour Medan Citra garden
5 minutes to the Inner ringroad south field
10 minutes to H. Adam Malik Hospital
10 minutes to North Sumatra University
15 minutes to the Amplas Toll gatee..
